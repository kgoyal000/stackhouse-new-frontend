Fine Art Papers

Hot Press Bright

Epson

A smooth, matte finish and bright white surface define Hot Press Bright, making it perfect for giclee prints that demand color vibrancy and sharp detail. It’s a top pick for artists and photographers alike.

Smooth Matte | 300gsm | 16 mil | 100% Cotton Rag

Cold Press Bright

Epson

With its textured finish and bright white tone, Cold Press Bright offers an exceptional base for fine art works requiring depth and intensity. Ideal for reproductions of watercolor paintings and fine detail artwork.

Textured Matte | 305gsm | 19 mil | 100% Cotton Rag

Photo Rag

Hahnemuehle

Combining a velvety matte surface with outstanding color reproduction, Photo Rag is versatile, making it suitable for detailed art and photography. Its 100% cotton base adds a luxurious feel to every print.

Textured Matte | 308 gsm | 18.9 mil | 100% Cotton Fiber

William Turner

Hahnemuehle

William Turner’s distinct texture mimics traditional watercolor paper, adding character and depth to art reproductions. Made from 100% cotton, it provides a unique tactile experience, perfect for high-end artworks.

Textured Matte | 310 gsm | 24.4 mil | 100% Cotton Fiber

FineArt Baryta

Hahnemuehle

Known for its glossy finish and baryta coating, Fine Art Baryta enhances black and color densities, offering high detail sharpness. It’s the go-to choice for high contrast images, black and white photography, and exhibition prints.

Textured Glossy | 325 gsm | 16.14 mil | 100% Alpha Cellulose

Fine Art Border Options

Enhance your artwork with our border options, added beyond your image's dimensions for the perfect finish.

Borderless: Your image reaches every print edge, offering a modern-looking, uninterrupted visual experience.

1/4-Inch Additional Border: Adds a slight edge around your image, providing an elegant separation between the artwork and its surrounding space.

1-Inch Additional Border: Enhances the visual presentation of your artwork and adds depth. It’s a perfect choice if you intend to display the print without framing or if you need more space for signing.

What is Giclee Printing?

Giclee printing refers to top-tier prints crafted with cutting-edge inkjet printers, archival inks, and premium media. Its unmatched quality and durability make it the ideal choice for exquisite fine art and photo reproductions.

How are our giclee prints crafted?

We employ the state-of-the-art Epson SureColor P9570, equipped with UltraChrome PRO12 Ink (12-color) for an expansive color range. Paired with premium media from top-tier paper manufacturers, our giclee prints boast vibrant hues and impressive lightfastness.

FAQs About Our Fine Art Prints

What makes Fine Art Prints different from standard prints?

Fine Art Prints at The Stackhouse are printed on superior, museum-quality, acid-free papers using archival inks. This combination ensures exceptional detail, durability, and color fidelity, making them ideal for artists and collectors.

Can I choose the type of paper for my Fine Art Print?

Yes, we offer a selection of fine art papers, including Hot Press Bright, Cold Press Bright, Photo Rag, William Turner, and Fine Art Baryta, each with unique textures and finishes to complement your artwork.

How should I handle and display Fine Art Prints?

Handle your prints with care, ideally with cotton gloves, and frame them behind UV-protective glass to prevent fading. Keep them out of direct sunlight and in stable temperature and humidity conditions.

Are Fine Art Prints suitable for all types of images?

Absolutely! Whether it’s photography, digital art, or traditional mediums like watercolor and oil, our Fine Art Prints can beautifully reproduce a wide range of artworks.

What sizes are available for Fine Art Prints?

We offer standard sizes from small to large and provide custom sizing options. Whether you need a compact 4x4" or a large-scale print, we can accommodate your specific requirements.

How long do Fine Art Prints last?

Our Fine Art Prints are designed to last over a century without significant fading when properly cared for, thanks to the archival quality of our inks and papers.

Is it possible to print limited editions of my artwork?

Certainly! We can work with you to create limited edition runs of your artwork, including numbering and if required, providing a certificate of authenticity.

What is the process for ensuring color accuracy in Fine Art Prints?

Our printing process includes regular calibration of printers and using color profiles that ensure the closest match to the original artwork. We recommend providing files in RGB for the best color accuracy.

What is the turnaround time for fine art prints?

Our standard turnaround time for fine art prints is 3 business days for printing. Shipping times vary based on your chosen method and location. For specific timelines or rush orders, please contact us directly.

What if my Fine Art Print doesn’t meet my expectations?

At The Stackhouse, your satisfaction is our top priority. If your print doesn’t meet your expectations, please reach out to us within 14 days of receipt. While we don't offer pre-print proofs, we're committed to working with you to resolve any issues and ensure your complete satisfaction with the final product.
